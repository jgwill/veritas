# 🔥🌊 South-West — The Threshold Between Analysis and Reflection

> Where structural thinking questions live. The South (analysis) has produced PDE decompositions. The West (reflection/validation) hasn't begun yet. This threshold is where we pause to ask: *Is the picture complete? What did we miss? Where are the discrepancies?*

## What lives here

- Structural thinking questions generated from PDE facets
- The four question types: Information, Clarification, Implication, Discrepancy
- Cross-facet pattern observations

## Why south-west?

Structural thinking sits between analysis and validation. It's not yet action (north), not yet full reflection (west), but it's past pure decomposition (east→south). It's the place where the agent's work gets examined before the MMOT evaluator (west) runs.

This is the pde-review-companion's territory.
