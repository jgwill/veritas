# 🌅 East (Waabinong) — Vision

*What veritas aspires to become.*

---

## The Aspiration

Veritas exists because honest assessment is rare and difficult. Most evaluation systems drift toward comfort — they soften language, avoid naming what isn't working, resolve tensions before they've been fully understood. Veritas refuses that drift.

### MMOT as Honest Assessment Practice

The Managerial Moment of Truth isn't a scoring rubric. It's a practice of courage: looking at current reality without flinching, naming the gap between what is and what was intended, and choosing action that advances rather than oscillates. When applied to software development — to the DigitalModels that represent decisions and performance reviews — MMOT becomes a way of holding ourselves accountable to what we said we'd build.

### The Courage to Say "Not Acceptable"

The hardest gift veritas offers is the word *no*. Not acceptable. Current reality does not match intended outcome. The evaluation is not honest. This is the east-facing vision: a tool ecosystem where glossing over is structurally impossible, where the four-step MMOT review (Acknowledge → Analyze → Plan → Feedback) is embedded in the workflow, not optional.

### Enabling Autonomous Evaluation

The horizon veritas walks toward: LLMs that can autonomously generate and evaluate Performance Review Models with the same rigor a skilled manager brings to a Moment of Truth conversation. Not replacing human judgment — augmenting it. Making the practice accessible at scale.

### Truth-and-Trust as Foundation

Every tool veritas builds — MCP server, CLI, evaluation engine — rests on one operating principle: truth first, then trust follows. You cannot trust an evaluation that avoids naming current reality. You cannot build trust in a system that performs assessment without practicing it.

### Bridges Between Human and AI

MCP and CLI are not just interfaces. They are bridges — between human evaluation practice and AI capability, between the manager who knows what honest assessment feels like and the model that can apply it consistently across hundreds of DigitalElements. The vision is convergence: human wisdom flowing through machine capability, machine consistency supporting human courage.
